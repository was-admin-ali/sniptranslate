Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Contact: aliamazn98@gmail.com

💜 We want you to be happy with SnipTranslate. If something isn't right, contact us within 14 days and we'll make it right.

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

  • 14-day money-back guarantee: If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within 14 days of the original purchase date.
  • Technical issues: If the Extension fails to work as described and we are unable to resolve the issue within a reasonable time, you are eligible for a full refund regardless of the time elapsed.
  • Duplicate charges: If you were charged more than once for the same plan, we will refund the duplicate charge immediately.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Refund requests made more than 14 days after the purchase date (except in cases of technical failure)
  • Refunds for partial subscription periods after cancellation
  • Purchases where the Extension was used extensively and no technical issue exists

3. Subscriptions and Cancellations

Monthly and Annual subscriptions renew automatically. You may cancel your subscription at any time through Paddle's customer portal. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you will retain Pro access until then.

Cancelling a subscription does not automatically issue a refund. To request a refund, please contact us directly.

4. Lifetime Plans

Lifetime purchases are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase. After 14 days, lifetime purchases are non-refundable as access is granted permanently.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at aliamazn98@gmail.com with:

  • Your order number or the email used for purchase
  • The reason for your refund request

We will process your request within 3–5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Paddle.

6. Contact

For any questions about refunds, please contact us at aliamazn98@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
