Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Contact: aliamazn98@gmail.com

🔒 SnipTranslate does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Text you translate is sent directly to translation APIs and is never stored on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

SnipTranslate collects minimal data necessary to operate the service:

  • Text you select for translation: Sent directly to third-party translation APIs (Google Translate, MyMemory). We do not store or log this text.
  • Images/screenshots for OCR: Sent to OCR.space API for text recognition. We do not store these images.
  • Usage count: Stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local) to enforce the free tier daily limit. This never leaves your device.
  • Language preferences: Stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.sync) for convenience.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers (unless you contact us directly)
  • We do not track your browsing history or the websites you visit
  • We do not use analytics or tracking scripts
  • We do not sell or share any data with advertisers

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Google Translate API: Used for text translation. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • MyMemory API: Fallback translation service. Subject to MyMemory's Privacy Policy.
  • OCR.space API: Used for image text recognition (Pro feature). Subject to OCR.space's Privacy Policy.
  • Paddle: Payment processor for Pro subscriptions. Subject to Paddle's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

All user preferences and usage data are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. This data does not leave your browser except as described in Section 3.

5. Children's Privacy

SnipTranslate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at aliamazn98@gmail.com.
